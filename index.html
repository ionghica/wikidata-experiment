Wikidata Experiment 1.0

Hi! My name is Ion (John) and I'm a Knowledge Base Construction enthusiast. This is a simple presentation page for an experiment I'm currently working on. What it essentially does is use the Mistral 7B Instruct v0.3 model to generate answers for specific questions like "Who are the children of Johann Sebastian Bach?" or "How many actors played in the movie Titanic?" using the internal knowledge of the LLM and data available on Wikidata.
